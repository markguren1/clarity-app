The Execution Formula

GOAL – What outcome must be produced?

ROLE – What expert perspective should AI adopt?

TASK – What must be executed?

FORMAT – How should results be structured?

CONSTRAINTS – What limits define success?

Example: Resume Positioning

Weak Prompt:
“Rewrite my resume to sound better.”

Result: Generic. Fluffy. No differentiation.

Structured Direction:

GOAL: Strengthen executive positioning
ROLE: Senior career strategist
TASK: Rewrite bullets to emphasize measurable AI-assisted results
FORMAT: Concise performance bullets
CONSTRAINTS: Each bullet must include outcome + metric

Result:

• Reduced reporting time 40% by implementing AI-assisted workflow automation
• Improved renewal rate 18% by restructuring analysis process
• Cut proposal turnaround from 5 days to 1 day using structured AI brief templates
